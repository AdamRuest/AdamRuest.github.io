About Me




I have now been working physically and internally with computers for over 5 years now and am ready for a real job in the domain. I've learned a lot, both from my studies and from my personal learnings and think i got most of it undr control. I have learned about programmation in high school on the school calculators, and got in trouble by coding the game "space invaders" entirely in the calculator. After that small experience, I got interested in real game design and began lerning C# to get myself used with the unity game development engine. I then decided to go further in programmation by beginning studies in a programming oriented school in Rimouski, close to my home town, in which i learned to use HTML, CSS, TypeScript and C++ so far.

While I learned a lot in in the programmation aspect of the job, I've also been learning by myself on the computer building part. I've began building computers back in my third year of high school. My first computer was a basic build on which I could only spend upwards of one thousand canadian dollar on. I then began upgrading that same computer for many years and it wasn't until I finished high school that i built my second computer. This time tho, i had more money so I spent more on an AIO cooling system, multiple SSDs and overall better parts. I've had this computer for over a year now and have been upgrading it at a constant pace ever since i got it. Although I'm better and like more the programming aspect of things, I also like a lot the computer building part and find it relaxing to do, giving me an activity to do that i enjoy and can find satisfaction in. I'd be more than happy to have a job in the programming aspect of things, I wouldn't mind finding a job in the computer building part and would take the job in a heartbeat.
